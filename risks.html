Risks

As we enjoy the winter season and all the fun activities it has to offer, it's important to remember that each activity comes with its own set of potential dangers, and it's vital to be mindful of these risks and take necessary precautions.

  • Snow and ice can make surfaces slippery, increasing the risk of falls, slips, and trips. To prevent these injuries it's important to wear proper footwear, take short steps, and use handrails when available.
  • Prolonged exposure to cold can lead to hypothermia and frostbite. To prevent such conditions it's important to dress in warm layers, stay dry, and take frequent breaks to warm up!
  • Skiing, Snowboarding, and ice skating can be espesically dangerous for those who are beginners. Potential injuires include sprains, fractures, and head injuries. To prevent such disasters it's important to wear proper safety gear, warm up properly, and take lessons from a qualified instructor.
